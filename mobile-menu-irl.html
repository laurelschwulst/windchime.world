Our in-person “open call” festival took place on Saturday, May 21st in NYC from 11am – 4pm in the Vale of Cashmere, Prospect Park, Brooklyn.


"Anyone and everyone is invited to make and bring their own wind chime, or just come and hang amongst the chimes. We can’t wait to see and hear what you all have made."

You can experience documentation from the event on the desktop version of this website, http://windchime.world. In the meantime, here is a video sharing some footage.

In case you're curious, here is the original call for the event as a Google Doc.
